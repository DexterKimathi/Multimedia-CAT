Why This Matters

Our planet's wildlife is disappearing at a rate 1,000 times faster than the natural background extinction rate. Habitat destruction, illegal poaching, and a warming climate are erasing species before we even discover them. This footage captures both the fragile beauty of what remains and the dedicated field teams working to turn the tide. Watch. Share. Act.
